What is a Dental Laboratory?

Dental laboratories make or customize a variety of products that are used by dentists to provide oral health care. The products include crowns, bridges, dentures, and other dental products. When fabricating these items, dental lab technicians follow a prescription from a licensed dentist. This includes prosthetic items (such as dentures and implants), and therapeutic items (such as orthodontic devices).

Crowns, bridges, dentures, and dental implants are among the most common restorations manufactured. In the field of dentistry, dental implants are among the most advanced technologies.
